Console

Console is an AUv3 audio effect for iOS that adds console-style saturation and distortion. Use it on synth or drum bus tracks to add warmth, punch, and cohesive analog character.

Quick start

  1. Open your Audio Unit host.
  2. Add Console as an AUv3 audio effect to your track.
  3. Choose a mode: Synth Bus for pads and leads, Drum Bus for drums and bass.
  4. Adjust DRIVE to add saturation, use MIX to blend with dry signal.
Note: Console processes audio. Insert it directly on an audio or instrument track.

Knob guide

MODE
Drum Bus for drums/bass, Synth Bus for pads/leads
DRIVE
Input gain and distortion intensity
CHARACTER
Softer (low) to firmer (high) saturation tone
MIX
Wet/dry blend for parallel processing
OUTPUT
Final volume trim for level matching

Common questions

  • Too much distortion: Lower MIX before lowering DRIVE to preserve the tone.
  • Muddy low end: Enable HPF and adjust TIGHT to tighten the bass.
  • Harsh highs: Lower BITE or reduce CHARACTER.
  • High CPU usage: Set OS to Off or Eco for lighter processing.
  • Latency: OS Std adds ~12 samples, HQ adds ~14. Use Eco or Off for minimum latency.
