Terrarum Privacy policy

Privacy Policy

Terrarum apps are designed to run locally. This page describes what data is collected and how it is used.

Data collection

Terrarum apps do not collect, store, or transmit personal data. No analytics, tracking, advertising, or third party telemetry services are used.

Network access

The apps do not require network access for core functionality.

Support email

If you contact support, your email address and the content of your message are used only to respond to your inquiry.
