Privacy Policy

Terrarum apps are designed to run locally. This policy describes what data is collected and how it is handled.

Data Collection

Terrarum apps do not collect, store, or transmit personal data. No analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party telemetry services are used.

Network Access

The apps do not require network access for core functionality.

Support Email

If you contact support, your email address and message content are used only to respond to your request.
