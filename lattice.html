Lattice

Lattice is an AUv3 MIDI instrument for iOS. Use it inside an Audio Unit host and route its MIDI output to a synth.

Quick start

  1. Open your Audio Unit host.
  2. Add Lattice as an AUv3 MIDI instrument or MIDI effect, depending on the host.
  3. Add a synth and route MIDI from Lattice to the synth.
  4. Press play in the host.
Note: Lattice is MIDI only. It will not produce audio unless routed to a synth.

Common questions

  • No sound: confirm Lattice is routed to a synth track.
  • Pattern changes unexpectedly: check Random controls and any host automation.
  • Saved state differs between hosts: preset persistence can be host specific.
